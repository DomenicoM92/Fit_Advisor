Why FitAdvisor?

People want to access a set of exercises from which to choose consciously…​ ..they are concerned about what kind of injuries, working on a specific muscle group could cause…​ ..they look for a good workout routine based on the muscle group of interest…​ ..more and more people are interested in working out at home, so they want to buy the necessary equipment in order to perform the exercises..​ What if exercises, routines, injuries and equipments were on the same platform?​
FitAdvisor solves all these problems!
FitAdvisor provides an easy to use user interface to have full access to:

  • an exercise dataset grouped by muscle groups
  • a list of equipments used in each exercise
  • a full set of workout routines divided by muscle group and equipments
  • a list of injuries by muscle group
  • an exhaustive description of each muscle group with images and explanations
